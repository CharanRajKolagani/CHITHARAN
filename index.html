About Me

Hi, I’m Charan Raj — a passionate and versatile visual artist specializing in photography, videography, and cinematography, with over 2 years of creative experience. My journey behind the lens has led me to capture a wide variety of stories, from the grace of classical dance events to the spontaneity of candid moments, fashion shoots, and model portfolios.

I’ve worked on engaging shoots with emerging YouTube influencers and artists, delivering visuals that truly reflect their personality and vision. Whether it's an event, a travel story, or a fashion project, I bring a personalized and professional approach to every shoot.

Equipped with a Nikon Z50, I also have access to high-end equipment on request, ensuring I meet the specific needs of any project. I’m open to freelance opportunities and always excited to collaborate, explore new concepts, and bring fresh ideas to life through my lens.
